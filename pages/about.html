More Than A Business, A Family Legacy

A Tradition of Taste

Founded by Chef Coraline Renaud, The Gilded Ladle is the culmination of a lifelong passion for food that connects people. Growing up surrounded by the rich aromas of her grandmother's Provençal kitchen, Coraline learned early that a shared meal is one of life’s greatest pleasures. Her mission is to recreate that feeling of warmth, community, and exquisite flavor for each and every client.
